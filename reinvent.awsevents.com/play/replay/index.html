re:Play Party

Thursday THURSDAY, NOV. 30 | 8:00PM 12:00AM

Not just an EDM concert and interactive extravaganza, the re:Play Party is the ultimate playground for those of us who didnt get the memo that grown-ups dont play. Watch last years recap video.

No backpacks or large bags will be allowed at the re:Play Party. Only one small bag, or wallet per person is allowed. The bag or wallet cannot exceed 4.5 by 6.5 with or without a handle/strap.

In addition, the following items are restricted from any re:Invent activity.

  • Functional firearms (including, but not limited to, airsoft guns, BB guns, cap guns, paintball guns and pellet guns)
  • Realistic replica firearms (including reproduction, fake or toy guns)
  • Functional projectile weapons (even toys)
  • Sharpened metal-bladed weapons including knives of any type (including pocket or utility knives of any size)
  • Explosives (including firecrackers and fireworks)
  • Chemical weapons (including mace and pepper spray)
  • Blunt weapons (including brass knuckles, clubs and nunchaku)
  • Instruments that cause excessive noise levels like air horns
  • Banners, pamphlets or material deemed disruptive to the conference
  • Personal drones or UAVs
  • Anything deemed inappropriate or unsafe for the venue by AWS in its sole discretion
